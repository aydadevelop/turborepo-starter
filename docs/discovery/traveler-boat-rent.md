# Role: Traveler In Boat Rent

## Mental Model

I am not just buying an experience.

I am reserving access to a scarce asset with safety, logistics, and weather risk.

I think in:

- boat type
- captain or self-drive
- capacity
- duration
- marina / departure point
- deposit and payment terms
- weather and cancellation

## Core Job To Be Done

Help me confidently answer:

- what boat this is
- what kind of outing it supports
- whether I need a captain or license
- how long I can rent it
- how much I pay now and later
- where we start and end

## Key Decision Questions

- Is this self-drive or captained?
- How many people does it really fit?
- What is included?
- What is the deposit or prepayment?
- What happens in bad weather?
- What are the cancellation and shift terms?
- How fixed is the departure point and time?

## Trust Triggers

- clear vessel photos
- captain/license clarity
- duration and pricing transparency
- marina/departure clarity
- weather and cancellation explanation
- deposit and damage expectations
- operator credibility

## Failure Triggers

- unclear captain requirement
- vague or hidden deposit terms
- unclear duration rules
- no departure location context
- weak asset presentation
- no operational confidence signals

## Objects This User Thinks In

- marina/location
- vessel
- charter package
- duration
- captain option
- deposit
- booking
- weather or reschedule condition

## Happy Path

1. Pick city or marina.
2. Browse vessels by capacity, style, and duration.
3. Understand captain/self-drive rules.
4. Understand deposit and payment timing.
5. Reserve a date and duration.
6. Confirm operational details.

## Playwright Exploration Missions

### Mission 1: leisure planner

- Can I quickly find a boat for my group size and vibe?
- Are the asset photos and vessel facts strong enough?

### Mission 2: cautious renter

- Can I tell exactly what I pay now, later, and as deposit?
- Can I understand weather/cancellation risk before booking?

### Mission 3: first-time renter

- Can I understand whether I need a captain or license?
- Can I tell what operational details will be confirmed later?

## What To Inspect In Our Product

- boat listing cards and gallery quality
- duration-first pricing surfaces
- deposit/prepayment communication
- captain/self-drive choice clarity
- marina/location detail
- booking flow structure for asset-based reservations

## First Red Tests We Should Eventually Have

- renter can distinguish captained vs self-drive before opening checkout
- renter can see duration pricing and deposit terms before booking
- renter can understand departure location and weather/cancellation rules on the PDP
